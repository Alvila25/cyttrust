About CytTrust Chad

CytTrust Chad is a private cybersecurity firm based in N’Djamena, founded by ISC2-certified professionals.

We serve enterprises, NGOs, financial institutions, and government agencies across Chad and Central Africa.

  • Mission: To secure our region’s digital infrastructure with expert-driven cybersecurity solutions.
  • Vision: A digitally confident Chad where security enables innovation.
